By Abraham Godson Thursday, April 15, 2021
 A young man born on 12th November, 1992 into the family of Late Mr. Edet Asuquo Awanaodung, hail from Eyopifie Village Urue-Offong/Oruko Local government area.   He attended Akwa Ibom State Polytechnic during his Ordinary National Diploma (OND) and later proceed to Heritage Polytechnic Eket to complete his Higher National Diploma (HND) in Computer Science.  He is a Teacher, a Web Developer and a Computer Scientist. He is the CEO of AB Technologies, a platform created to empower youth within the city and nation at large.
